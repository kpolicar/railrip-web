Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2025  ·  Last updated: 1 June 2025
RailRip is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, Regulation (EU) 2016/679). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is RailRip (contact: privacy@railrip.cloud). For data protection queries or to exercise your rights, please use this address.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account data — email address, password hash, plan details, billing address;
  • Payment data — processed by our PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor; we do not store full card numbers;
  • Usage data — API call logs, session timestamps, error reports, IP address;
  • Technical data — browser type, operating system, referrer URL (collected automatically for security and analytics).

3. Legal Basis & Purposes

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — to provide and operate the RailRip service, process payments, and send transactional emails;
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — for fraud prevention, security monitoring, and service improvement;
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR) — to comply with tax, accounting, and other legal requirements;
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — for optional marketing communications you opt in to.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party processors strictly necessary to operate the service (payment processors, cloud hosting providers, email delivery services). All processors are bound by data processing agreements and operate under adequate data protection guarantees.

Where processors are located outside the EEA, appropriate safeguards (such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

5. Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription and for up to 3 years thereafter to comply with legal obligations. Usage logs are retained for 90 days. You may request deletion of your account data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject you have the following rights:

Right of access to your data
Right to rectification
Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
Right to restriction of processing
Right to data portability
Right to object to processing

To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@railrip.cloud. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

7. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and session management. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used. Analytics cookies (if any) are anonymised and require your consent.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including TLS encryption in transit, bcrypt password hashing, and regular security audits. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be communicated by email or via your dashboard at least 30 days before taking effect. The latest version is always available at this URL.

10. Contact

For privacy-related queries: privacy@railrip.cloud
