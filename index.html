Built on Academic Foundations

[cite_start]

RailRip's architecture is inspired by the thesis Exploiting Third-party Software To Bypass Anti-cheat Detection Systems Through Network-driven Development by Klemen Janez Poličar[cite: 10, 11].

[cite_start]

The research explores the concept of entirely decoupling cheat software from the host computer[cite: 315]. [cite_start]By moving the execution out of bounds of traditional ring-0 anti-cheat scanners, the method inherently voids currently known protection techniques[cite: 316, 227]. [cite_start]While not peer-reviewed, this research forms the theoretical backbone of our remote approach, allowing developers to escape the endless exploit-patch lifecycle[cite: 171, 125].

Hosted in the Cloud


RailRip is built for cheat developers who want to decouple their software from the user's machine. Ship your software to our cloud (or bring your own hardware). We take care of the rerouting and packet management. You just subscribe to events via our API.

Zero Local Footprint

The Result: The end user doesn't run any illegitimate software on their laptop. [cite_start]The third-party VNC acts as a trojan horse[cite: 357]. All they need is standard VNC software and our auto-configured VPN. [cite_start]Because your logic runs remotely, you drastically reduce the need to patch your cheat when the game updates[cite: 125].

The Bypass Engine under the Hood
